Luke Skywalker - The Chosen One

Luke Skywalker was an ordinary man, who lived on the planet Tatooine, accompanied by his aunt and uncle, who were humble farmers. One day, he meets a man known as Ben Kenobi, who teaches young Skywalker of the force, a mysterious power only known by a select few. Luke learns of the Jedi, an ancient order of warriors who used the force to fight back against evil. Luke joins the rebel alliance, a resistance group who fight to take down the empire, and the Sith Lord Darth Vader. Throughout his journey, Luke is joined by two droids, a smuggler, a princess, Kenobi and a Wookie.

Leia Organa - The Princess

Leia Organa hails from the planet Alderaan, and has the title of princess. She is one of the head figures of the resistance, who are the rebel forces fighting back against the Empire's oppression. Leia is kidnapped by Darth Vader and leaves a message for Obi-Wan-Kenobi -- this is where it all begins.

Han Solo - The Smuggler

Han Solo is a smuggler who travels the galaxy with his partner Chewbacca aboard the Milleneum Falcon, the fastest ship in the galaxy. He is an outlaw, who is willing to bend the rules if the price is right. Not only is he a smuggler, he is a con artist and merceanary, but deep down he has a good heart.

X

Chewbacca - The Wookie

Chewbacca is a Wookie -- a type of alien, who is a smuggler alongside Han Solo. Chewbacca is the co-pilot of the Milleneum Falcon, and is with Solo every step of the way. He uses a high-powered crossbow that fires explosive lasers at his enemies. Although he doesn't speak English, Han Solo can still understand what Chewbacca says; their friendship is one that runs deep.

Obi Wan Kenobi - The Jedi Knight

Back in the day, Obi Wan Kenobi was a renowned Jedi Knight. Now he wanders as Old Ben Kenobi, keeping his distance from the empire. Obi Wan is one of the last Jedi to remain, and is a powerful wielder of the force. He takes Luke under his wing, and teaches him many things about the Jedi, as well as Luke's potential to master the force and wield it for himself. He also gives Luke a lightsaber, a weapon wielded by the Jedi.

X

Darth Vader - The Sith Lord

Darth Vader is a Sith Lord, one of the heads of the Empire. He is a tyrannical leader who seeks to crush the resistance with brute power. Like the Jedi, Vader can weild the force, but he uses it for evil. he wields a lightsaber that glows red, signifying his status among the empire. He was formerly a Jedi, but turned towards the dark side of the force, where power is sought by any means. Darth Vader is also one of the most skilled pilots in the galaxy.
